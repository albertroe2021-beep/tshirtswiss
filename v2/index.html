Swiss-Managed Manufacturing

Swiss-Managed Clothing Manufacturing for Businesses Across Switzerland

TShirtSwiss.ch was built for Swiss businesses that want professional clothing manufacturing without losing control of communication, quality and delivery. We combine Swiss-managed project handling with apparel production in Thailand.

Our team helps define the product, confirm fabrics, review artwork, organise sampling, manage production, check quality and prepare orders for international delivery.

Whether you need staff uniforms, private label clothing, sports team apparel, promotional merchandise or a repeat production programme, our process is designed to make international manufacturing easier.

The result is a Swiss-managed clothing manufacturer based in Thailand, serving Switzerland with the quality mindset, communication and reliability Swiss buyers expect.
