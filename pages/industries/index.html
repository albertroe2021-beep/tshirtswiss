Industry-Specific Planning

Apparel Built Around the Way It Will Be Used

A good apparel manufacturer does not recommend the same garment for every client. A hotel uniform, ecommerce hoodie, healthcare uniform, construction polo and fightwear short each have different performance requirements.

Who will wear the garment? How often will it be washed? Does it need stretch, breathability, durability, premium appearance or retail packaging? These details influence fabric choice, fit, decoration method, finishing and packing.

By combining industry understanding with Swiss-managed production in Thailand, TShirt Swiss helps clients produce apparel that is not only branded but also fit for purpose.
