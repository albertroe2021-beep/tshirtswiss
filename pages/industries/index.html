Industry-Specific Planning

Apparel Built Around the Way It Will Be Used

A good apparel project starts with use case. Who will wear the garment? How often will it be washed? Does it need stretch, breathability, durability, premium appearance, role identification or retail packaging?

These details influence fabric choice, fit, decoration method, finishing and packing. By combining industry understanding with Swiss-managed production in Thailand, TShirt Swiss helps clients produce apparel that is not only branded but also fit for purpose.

  • Corporate and hospitality uniforms
  • Healthcare and wellness apparel
  • Construction and trade workwear
  • Ecommerce and private label collections
  • Creator merchandise and events
  • Sportswear and combat sports products
