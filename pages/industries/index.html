Industry-Specific Planning

Apparel Built Around the Way It Will Be Used

A good apparel manufacturer does not recommend the same garment for every client. A hotel uniform, ecommerce hoodie, healthcare tunic, construction polo, school camp t-shirt and technical sports short each have different requirements.

Who will wear the garment? How often will it be washed? Does it need stretch, breathability, durability, premium appearance, role identification or retail packaging? These details influence fabric choice, fit, decoration method, finishing and packing.

By combining industry understanding with Swiss-managed production in Thailand, TShirt Swiss helps clients produce apparel that is not only branded but also fit for purpose.

Common Requirements by Industry

What We Help Buyers Plan

Each industry has different priorities, but most successful apparel projects require the same planning discipline.

Garment Selection

Choosing the right mix of t-shirts, polos, hoodies, caps, uniforms, sportswear or merchandise products.

Fabric and Climate

Selecting materials around heat, movement, wash frequency, durability, stretch and perceived value.

Branding Method

Choosing embroidery, screen printing, sublimation, heat transfer, labels or packaging based on the product.

Distribution Planning

Organising size ratios, department packing, team packing, branch packing or ecommerce-ready preparation.
