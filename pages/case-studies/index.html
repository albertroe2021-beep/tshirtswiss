Project Examples

How We Support Different Apparel Requirements

Corporate Uniform Rollout

A corporate apparel project usually requires consistent sizing, professional colours, durable decoration and reliable repeat ordering. We help define garment types, branding placement, embroidery or print methods, packing and delivery so multiple teams receive a consistent final product.

Private Label T-Shirt Collection

For a private label brand, the garment must feel retail-ready. We support fabric selection, fit direction, sampling, custom labels, hang tags, packaging and repeat production planning so the collection can be presented professionally online or in store.

Sportswear and Fightwear Production

Performance apparel requires careful fabric and construction choices. For rashguards, training tops, MMA shorts and Muay Thai shorts, we focus on movement, durability, colour impact, sublimation options and quality checks suited to active use.

Hospitality and Healthcare Uniforms

Uniforms for hospitality and healthcare must balance comfort, appearance and washability. We help clients select garments that look professional while remaining practical for daily use across front-of-house, clinical or service teams.

Event Merchandise Campaign

Event apparel requires clear deadlines, strong visual branding and dependable delivery. We support t-shirts, tote bags, caps, hoodies and promotional merchandise for conferences, launches, festivals and corporate campaigns.

Workwear for Field Teams

Trade and construction apparel must be practical, durable and brand consistent. We help plan polos, t-shirts, hoodies, jackets and caps that support team identity while standing up to everyday use.

Operational Excellence

From Brief to Delivery, Every Detail Matters

The strongest apparel projects are not created by guessing. They are created through clear specifications, suitable materials, realistic timelines and careful production control. Our Swiss-managed approach gives clients a structured process for turning a product idea into finished garments that meet commercial expectations.

Before production begins, we clarify garment purpose, quantity, fabric, sizing, branding, packaging, quality expectations and shipping destination. During production, we manage the details that affect the final result: measurements, stitching, print or embroidery placement, colour consistency, labels, finishing and packing. This is how we help clients reduce risk and build confidence in their manufacturing partner.

If your organisation is planning a custom apparel project, TShirt Swiss can help you define the best production route and avoid the common problems associated with unclear overseas sourcing.
