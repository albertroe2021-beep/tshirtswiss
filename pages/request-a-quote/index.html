What to Include in Your Quote Request

A clear enquiry helps us understand the product, production complexity and delivery requirements. Apparel pricing depends on quantity, fabric, garment construction, decoration method, labels, packaging, sampling needs and shipping destination.

We recommend including the following details where available:

  • Garment type, such as t-shirt, polo, hoodie, uniform, rashguard, scrub, workwear or tote bag.
  • Estimated quantity per style, colour and size.
  • Fabric preference, GSM, performance requirement or reference garment.
  • Logo, artwork, print, embroidery or sublimation details.
  • Custom labels, hang tags, packaging or private label requirements.
  • Target delivery date and shipping destination.
  • Any existing sample, tech pack, size chart or brand guideline.
