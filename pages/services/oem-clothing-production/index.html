Reliable Garment Manufacturing from Your Specifications

OEM clothing production is ideal when you already know what you want to make and need a reliable manufacturing partner to produce it professionally. You may have a sample, tech pack, size chart, fabric reference, branding requirements or an existing garment you want to reproduce with improvements.

Built for Professional Buyers

From Approved Sample to Bulk Apparel Production

OEM manufacturing gives your business more control than choosing a ready-made blank garment. It allows you to define fabric, fit, sizing, construction, trims, labels, decoration and packing rules. This is useful for brands building retail products, companies that need consistent uniforms, distributors managing repeat orders and ecommerce labels that want garments aligned with their positioning.

Our role is to make the process clearer and easier to manage. We review your requirements, advise where specifications need to be more precise, coordinate sampling when needed and manage the production path through practical checkpoints.

  • Samples, references and tech packs
  • Fabric and trim planning
  • Screen print, embroidery and sublimation
  • Labels, hang tags and packaging
  • Bulk production coordination
  • Quality control and shipping support

Materials, Branding and Finishing

Production Details That Decide the Final Quality

Successful OEM production depends on decisions made before bulk manufacturing starts. Fabric weight, shrinkage, hand feel, stitching, trims, label placement, print method and packing rules all affect the result. We help customers think through these details early so the approved sample becomes a reliable foundation for the final order.

  • Custom sizing and measurement control
  • Screen printing and embroidery
  • Sublimation and heat transfer options
  • Neck labels, woven labels and care labels
  • Hang tags, polybags and carton packing

OEM FAQs

Common Questions About OEM Clothing Production

What does OEM clothing production mean?

OEM means garments are produced according to your specifications, samples or technical requirements rather than simply buying a standard blank item.

Do I need a tech pack?

A tech pack is helpful, but not always required. We can also work from samples, reference garments, size charts and detailed written requirements.

Can you help with sampling?

Yes. Sampling is recommended when fit, fabric, labels, decoration or construction details need to be confirmed before bulk production.

Can you produce private label clothing?

Yes. OEM production can include private label elements such as custom neck labels, woven labels, hang tags, packaging and retail presentation.

Which products can be made through OEM production?

We support t-shirts, polos, hoodies, jackets, uniforms, workwear, sportswear, rashguards, shorts, caps, bags and private label apparel ranges.

Can you ship to Switzerland?

Yes. Orders can be shipped to Switzerland, Europe and other international destinations depending on the production and delivery requirements.
