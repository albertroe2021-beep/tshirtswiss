The OEM Production Advantage

Reliable, Versatile and Built for Growing Apparel Programmes

OEM clothing production is ideal when you already know what you want to make and need a reliable manufacturing partner to produce it professionally. You may have a sample, a tech pack, a size chart, a fabric reference, branding requirements or an existing garment you want to reproduce with improvements.

At TShirtSwiss.ch, we combine Swiss quality standards with experienced Thai apparel manufacturing. Our team helps translate your product requirements into factory-ready production steps, covering materials, measurements, decoration, labels, packing and quality checks. From corporate apparel to private label collections, workwear, sportswear and merchandise, our OEM process is designed to keep your garments consistent from sample approval through bulk delivery.

OEM Manufacturing for Brands, Companies and Distributors

OEM production is different from choosing a ready-made blank garment. It gives your business more control over the final product, including fabric, fit, sizing, construction, colour, trims, labels, decoration and packing. This is useful for brands that want a unique product, companies that need consistent uniforms, distributors that need repeatable supply, and ecommerce labels that want garments aligned with their retail positioning.

Our role is to make the manufacturing process clearer and easier to manage. We help review your garment requirements, advise where specifications need to be more precise, and coordinate production with practical quality control checkpoints. Whether your order is a custom t-shirt programme, polo range, hoodie collection, workwear set, sportswear product or private label line, the aim is always the same: stable production, predictable results and garments that arrive ready for your team, customers or retail channel.

From Tech Pack to Factory-Ready Production

If you already have a tech pack, our team can use it as the foundation for production planning. If you only have a garment sample or reference idea, we can help identify the missing information needed to move forward. This may include fabric type, GSM, size measurements, tolerance levels, stitching details, decoration placement, label position, colour references and packing instructions.

The more complete the specification, the easier it is to keep production accurate. That is why OEM clothing production is especially valuable for repeat orders and brand programmes. Once a garment is approved, the same details can be documented for future production runs. This helps reduce variation, keeps your apparel consistent, and makes restocks or collection expansions much easier to manage.

Packing, Labelling and Delivery Planning

OEM production does not end when the garment is sewn or decorated. Packing and labelling are important parts of the final customer experience. Orders can be folded, sized, labelled, polybagged and packed according to your requirements. This is useful for companies distributing uniforms internally, ecommerce brands preparing retail products, and distributors managing multiple SKUs or branch deliveries.

Clear packing rules reduce confusion when products arrive. We can support packing by size, colour, SKU, department, employee kit, branch, event, product drop or carton label. Combined with quality control and shipping coordination, this helps make the final delivery easier to receive, sort and distribute.

FAQ

OEM Clothing Production Questions

What does OEM clothing production mean?

OEM production means garments are made according to your specifications, sample, design, tech pack or brand requirements rather than simply using a standard blank product.

Do I need a tech pack to start?

A tech pack is helpful, but not always required. You can also start with a physical sample, reference garment or product concept. We will advise what information is missing.

What garments can be produced through OEM?

OEM production can support t-shirts, polos, hoodies, workwear, uniforms, sportswear, shorts, caps, bags and private label apparel depending on the project.

Can you add custom labels and packaging?

Yes. OEM projects can include woven labels, printed labels, care labels, hang tags, stickers, polybags, carton labels and packing instructions.

Can you produce repeat orders?

Yes. Approved specifications can be documented so future production follows the same fabric, measurements, colour, branding and packing rules.

How long does OEM production take?

Timing depends on product complexity, sampling, materials, decoration and quantity. Once specifications are clear, we can advise a realistic production schedule.
