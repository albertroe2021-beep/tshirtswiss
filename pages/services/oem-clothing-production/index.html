OEM Production With Swiss Oversight

Manufacturing Your Apparel to Approved Specifications

OEM clothing production is different from generic apparel sourcing. The objective is not simply to find a blank garment. It is to manufacture your product according to agreed details: fabric, fit, construction, colours, decoration, labels, packaging and delivery requirements.

We help clients turn approved designs or reference samples into structured production orders. This includes checking whether specifications are clear, advising on production feasibility, confirming sample requirements and managing the transition from approval to bulk manufacturing.

Process

Our OEM Clothing Production Process

We follow a structured OEM workflow so each production stage is clear, documented and aligned with the approved garment specification.

1

Specification Review

We review your tech pack, sample, artwork, size chart, garment requirements and target quantity.

2

Feasibility and Quotation

We confirm production options, material requirements, decoration methods, timeline and pricing.

3

Sampling or Confirmation

Samples or reference approvals confirm fabric, fit, construction and branding before bulk production.

4

Material and Trim Coordination

We coordinate fabrics, labels, hang tags, packaging, trims and production inputs.

5

Bulk Manufacturing

Garments are produced according to the approved specification with in-process checks.

6

Inspection and Shipping

Finished goods are inspected, packed and prepared for delivery to your destination.
