Before Production

Sampling Reduces Risk and Improves Production Accuracy

Sampling helps confirm whether the garment matches the intended specification before production begins. It allows you to review fit, fabric, measurements, construction, decoration and finishing in a way that drawings or descriptions cannot fully show.

For brands, sampling protects customer experience. For companies ordering uniforms, it helps confirm comfort and presentation. For OEM buyers, it creates a production reference that can be checked against bulk manufacturing.

Process

Our Apparel Sampling Process

We manage sampling with clear approval points so you understand what is being tested and what needs feedback before production.

1

Sample Brief

We review the garment type, artwork, reference sample, fabric request, sizing and purpose.

2

Specification Confirmation

We clarify fabric, measurements, decoration, labels, packaging and construction details.

3

Sample Production

The sample is produced according to the agreed instructions and available materials.

4

Review and Feedback

You review fit, comfort, appearance, branding, finishing and any required changes.

5

Revision if Needed

If important changes are required, we adjust the specification or prepare another sample.

6

Bulk Approval

Once approved, the sample becomes the production reference for the bulk order.

What Samples Help Confirm

Fit, Fabric, Branding and Finishing

Each sample should answer practical questions before production begins. Does the fabric feel right? Does the garment fit the intended wearer? Is the logo size balanced? Are the labels and packaging suitable for the brand? Sampling helps answer these questions with a real garment.

Measurements and Fit

Review length, width, sleeve shape, shoulder balance, movement and size accuracy.

Fabric Performance

Check fabric weight, stretch, texture, breathability, opacity and wash expectations.

Decoration Placement

Confirm print, embroidery, sublimation, heat transfer or label positioning.

Retail Details

Review care labels, hang tags, neck prints, folding, packaging and presentation.
