Sample With Confidence

Review the Garment Before Production Starts

Sampling helps you make better decisions before moving into bulk manufacturing. A sample allows you to review the garment's fit, fabric, construction, decoration, label placement and finishing details in a physical product rather than relying only on sketches or specifications.

TShirtSwiss.ch supports apparel sampling for private label clothing, OEM production, corporate apparel, workwear, sportswear and merchandise. Our sampling process helps brands identify issues early, refine specifications and approve production with greater confidence.

Sampling for Private Label, OEM and Custom Apparel Projects

Every garment decision becomes more meaningful once you can see and touch a sample. Fabric weight, stretch, softness, opacity, colour, stitching and print placement all affect the final result. Sampling gives your team a clear reference for approval and helps reduce costly mistakes before bulk production.

Samples can be used to validate new product ideas, improve an existing garment, test decoration methods or confirm private label details. For repeatable apparel programmes, the approved sample becomes an important production benchmark that future orders can follow.

What We Check

Fit, Fabric, Construction and Branding Validation

A sample can reveal details that are difficult to judge on paper. The goal is to confirm that the product feels right, looks right and can be produced consistently.

Sample Checks

  • Measurements and fit proportions
  • Fabric weight, feel and performance
  • Stitching and construction details
  • Print, embroidery or transfer placement

Brand Details

  • Neck labels and care labels
  • Hang tags and size stickers
  • Packaging and folding method
  • SKU and carton labelling rules

Why Sampling Saves Time Before Bulk Manufacturing

Sampling is not just a visual check. It is a decision-making tool. It helps confirm whether the garment is aligned with the customer, price point and production method. If changes are needed, they can be made before the factory begins the full order.

For private label brands, the sample also supports photography, product page planning and packaging decisions. For corporate apparel or uniforms, it helps stakeholders approve the garment before ordering for a larger team. For sportswear and workwear, it helps test movement, comfort and suitability.

FAQ

Apparel Sampling Questions

Do I need a sample before production?

For custom apparel, private label products and OEM manufacturing, sampling is strongly recommended before bulk production.

What can be checked in a sample?

You can check fit, fabric, construction, decoration placement, labels, packaging and overall product quality.

Can a sample be revised?

Yes. Samples can be reviewed and revised where needed before production approval.

Can sampling support private label products?

Yes. Samples can include labels, hang tags, branding and packaging details for private label apparel.

Does a sample guarantee future consistency?

The approved sample becomes a reference for production, supported by documented specifications and quality checks.

How long does sampling take?

Timing depends on garment complexity, materials, branding and revision requirements. We can advise once the brief is clear.
