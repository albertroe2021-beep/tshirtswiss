Buyer Planning

Plan the Destination Before Production Ends

Shipping preparation works best when the delivery destination, receiving process and packing requirements are known early. This is especially important for multi-location uniform programmes, ecommerce product drops and time-sensitive events.

  • Confirm delivery destination and receiving contact details early.
  • Define whether goods should be packed by size, style, branch, event or customer group.
  • Plan carton labels around the receiving team’s needs.
  • Coordinate hang tags, stickers, labels and packaging before final packing.
  • Allow time for export documentation, freight handover and destination handling.
  • Check import rules, taxes and customs requirements with the destination-side provider where needed.
