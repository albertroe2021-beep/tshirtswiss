Global Delivery Support

From Factory Packing to International Shipment Preparation

Worldwide apparel shipping requires more than sending cartons out the door. Orders need to be packed clearly, labelled accurately, grouped logically and prepared for the selected delivery route.

TShirtSwiss.ch supports shipping preparation for private label apparel, corporate clothing, uniforms, sportswear, workwear and merchandise manufactured in Thailand. Our process connects production, quality control, packaging and delivery coordination so your order is easier to receive and distribute.

Shipping Support for Private Label, Uniform and Merchandise Orders

Different apparel orders require different shipping approaches. A private label ecommerce order may need SKU-based carton labelling and product-ready packing. A corporate uniform order may need cartons grouped by branch, department or employee kit. An event merchandise order may need delivery planned around a launch or event date.

Clear shipping preparation helps reduce delays and confusion. Carton labels, packing lists, size breakdowns, SKU grouping and export documentation coordination all help make the delivery smoother. When these details are considered early, the final handover is easier for both the production team and the receiving team.

Why Shipping Planning Matters Before Apparel Leaves the Factory

When shipping details are left until the end, orders can become harder to manage. Missing carton labels, unclear packing lists, mixed sizes or poorly grouped cartons can create extra work for the receiving team and delay distribution.

By planning shipping alongside packaging and quality control, apparel orders can leave the factory in a cleaner, more organised format. This is useful for ecommerce brands receiving inventory, corporate teams distributing uniforms, distributors handling multiple SKUs, and event organisers working against a fixed deadline.
