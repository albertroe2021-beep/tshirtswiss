Shipping Advantage

The Apparel Order Is Not Complete Until It Arrives Properly Packed

Worldwide shipping is the final stage of the apparel manufacturing workflow, but it should not be treated as an afterthought. A garment order can be well made and quality checked, yet still create problems for the buyer if cartons are poorly organised, packing lists are unclear, sizes are mixed incorrectly or the delivery route is not planned early enough.

For Swiss brands, corporate buyers, ecommerce stores, sports clubs, event organisers and private label apparel companies, shipping needs to support the way the order will be received and distributed. Some orders need cartons separated by style, size or colour. Others need packing by branch, department, team, player, SKU or retail assortment. For private label orders, packaging and shipping should also protect the product presentation created through labels, hang tags, polybags and carton organisation.

TShirtSwiss.ch supports worldwide apparel shipping from Thailand by connecting production, packaging, quality control and delivery planning. We help buyers think through carton rules, packing instructions, shipment timing, destination requirements and the practical information needed before the finished order leaves the production environment.

Shipment Preparation

What Should Be Confirmed Before Apparel Ships?

Before a finished apparel order is shipped, the buyer should confirm destination, delivery timing, carton structure, packing list expectations and any distribution requirements. A private label order for ecommerce may require SKU-level organisation. A corporate uniform order may require cartons separated by department or location. A sports club order may need packing by player, size or team.

The right shipping approach depends on the product and the buyer's receiving process. Bulk cartons may be suitable for simple orders, while individually packed garments, barcode stickers, size separation or carton-level labels may be useful for retail and ecommerce. Planning these details before dispatch can reduce sorting time and help avoid confusion after delivery.

  • Destination and delivery deadline
  • Carton labels and packing list requirements
  • Size, colour, SKU or branch separation
  • Private label packaging protection
  • Export and shipment coordination
  • Pre-dispatch quality and count checks

Switzerland, Europe and International Buyers

Shipping Should Match the Way the Order Will Be Used After Arrival

Some apparel orders go straight into storage. Others need to be distributed to stores, teams, branches, employees, customers or fulfilment centres. The best shipping plan depends on what happens after the cartons arrive. For Swiss corporate buyers, separation by department or location can be useful. For ecommerce brands, SKU and size organisation may be more important. For sports teams, packing by player or team group can save time.

When packing and shipping are planned alongside production, buyers can receive finished goods in a more useful format. This is especially valuable for large uniform programmes, private label launches, event merchandise, teamwear and repeat production where small packing errors can create a lot of manual work later.

  • Swiss and European delivery planning
  • Ecommerce and SKU organisation
  • Retail-ready packing support
  • Corporate branch or department sorting
  • Sports team and event distribution
  • Repeat order shipping standards

Worldwide Shipping FAQs

Common Questions About Apparel Shipping

Can apparel be shipped from Thailand to Switzerland?

Yes. We support shipping planning for apparel orders produced in Thailand and delivered to Switzerland, Europe and international destinations.

Can cartons be packed by size or colour?

Yes. Packing can be planned by size, colour, product type, SKU, department, branch, team or other receiving requirements.

Should packing be planned before production ends?

Yes. Packing and shipping requirements should be defined before final dispatch so the order can be organised correctly.

Can shipping include private label packaging?

Yes. Shipping can be coordinated with labels, hang tags, polybags, carton labels and retail presentation requirements.

Is quality control done before shipping?

Quality control is recommended before dispatch to check product consistency, counts, labels, packing and shipment readiness.

Can orders be prepared for ecommerce fulfilment?

Yes. Orders can be packed with SKU, size, colour and carton organisation to make ecommerce receiving and fulfilment easier.
