Connected Workflow

Services Should Work Together, Not in Isolation

Most apparel projects require more than one service. A private label range may need product development, sampling, OEM production, labels, hang tags, packaging, quality control and shipping. A sportswear order may need sublimation, heat transfer names, packing by team and international delivery.

  • Start with product direction and quantities
  • Approve samples before bulk production
  • Choose decoration and brand finishing early
  • Define QC, packaging and shipping requirements
