How Services Fit Together

Most Projects Need a Combination of Services

A simple staff shirt may only need product selection, printing and quality control. A private label sportswear range may need development, sampling, sublimation, custom labels, packaging and worldwide shipping.

Our role is to help you choose the right combination based on product type, quantity, branding, timeline and delivery destination.

  • OEM production for existing specifications
  • Product development for early concepts
  • Sampling before bulk production
  • Printing, embroidery or sublimation
  • Labels, hang tags and packaging
  • Quality control and shipment preparation

Need help choosing the right services?

Tell us your garment type, quantity, artwork, branding needs and delivery destination. We will recommend the service path that fits your project.
