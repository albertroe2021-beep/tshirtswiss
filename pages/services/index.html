Decoration Services

Printing and Embroidery for Custom Apparel

Decoration has a major effect on how a garment looks, feels and performs. A logo that works well as embroidery may not be suitable for a large print. A full-colour sportswear design may need sublimation rather than screen printing.

Our team helps clients select the most suitable decoration method based on garment type, fabric, artwork, quantity, durability requirements and budget. The goal is to create branded apparel that looks professional and remains appropriate for its intended use.

Screen Printing

Strong for custom t-shirts, uniforms, promotional apparel, band merchandise, event apparel and bold artwork at scale.
