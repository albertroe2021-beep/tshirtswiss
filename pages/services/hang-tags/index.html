Hang Tag Advantage

Hang Tags Help Apparel Feel Ready to Sell

Hang tags are a small finishing detail that can make a major difference to how an apparel product is perceived. They give the buyer a place to tell the brand story, show product information, communicate size or price, add a barcode, include a QR code, explain care details or create a more premium first impression.

For private label brands, ecommerce stores, gyms, creators, retailers and corporate buyers, hang tags help transform clothing from a manufactured garment into a product that is ready for sale, gifting or distribution. They also support internal organisation because tags can carry SKU references, colour names, size information or product codes that make fulfilment and inventory easier.

TShirtSwiss.ch helps buyers plan hang tags as part of a wider garment production and private label workflow. Hang tags can be connected with custom labels, packaging, OEM production, sampling and quality control so the final garment arrives as a complete branded product rather than a garment with finishing details added too late.

More Than Decoration

A Hang Tag Can Carry the Product Story, Retail Logic and Customer Instructions

Hang tags are not only visual branding. They can support sales, inventory, product education, care instructions, sustainability messaging and ecommerce presentation. The best tag is designed around both the brand and the way the garment will be sold or delivered.

Tag Planning

What Should Be Confirmed Before Producing Hang Tags?

Before hang tags are produced, the brand should confirm what the tag needs to do. Some tags are mainly visual and support the look of the product. Others need to include pricing, barcode information, QR codes, product descriptions, care messaging, size references or launch campaign details.

The attachment method also matters. Hang tags may use strings, pins, loops or other tag fastening methods depending on the garment and presentation. A retail hoodie, a folded t-shirt, a cap, a tote bag and a sportswear item may all need different tag positions so the product looks clean and the tag does not damage or interfere with the garment.

  • Logo, artwork and brand story
  • Tag size, shape and material
  • Barcode, QR code or SKU information
  • String, pin or attachment method
  • Tag placement by garment type
  • Quality control before packing

Retail and Ecommerce Readiness

Hang Tags Help Products Move Through Sales Channels

For ecommerce and retail, hang tags can be functional as well as visual. Barcodes, QR codes, SKU references, colour names and size details can make products easier to list, pack, scan, sort and sell. A tag can also direct customers to a website, care guide, product story, social media page or repeat purchase offer.

For private label brands, hang tags often work best when combined with custom labels and packaging. The label gives the garment a permanent identity, the hang tag explains the product or sale information, and the packaging completes the delivery experience.

  • Barcodes and SKU information
  • QR codes and product links
  • Retail price or product notes
  • Brand story and collection messaging
  • Custom labels and packaging alignment
  • QC before final packing

Hang Tag FAQs

Common Questions About Custom Hang Tags

What information can go on a hang tag?

Hang tags can include brand story, product details, price, barcode, SKU, QR code, care notes, size information or campaign messaging.

Can hang tags be added to private label clothing?

Yes. Hang tags are a common finishing detail for private label apparel, retail collections, ecommerce products and branded merchandise.

Do I need tag artwork?

A print-ready design is helpful, but the first step is deciding what the tag needs to communicate and how it should attach to the garment.

Can hang tags include barcodes or QR codes?

Yes. Barcodes, QR codes, SKU references and product links can be planned into the tag artwork where required.

Should hang tags be sampled first?

Sampling is recommended when tag material, attachment, placement or retail presentation needs approval before bulk production.

Can hang tags be combined with packaging?

Yes. Hang tags, custom labels, polybags, stickers and cartons can be planned together as a complete product presentation system.
