Shape and Branding

Choosing the Right Cap Structure and Decoration Method

Headwear success often comes down to shape. A corporate cap may need a subtle curved peak and clean embroidery, while a streetwear brand may prefer a structured cap with bold front branding. Event merchandise may prioritise strong visual impact, reliable fit and practical pricing.

Decoration method is equally important. Flat embroidery gives a clean professional finish, 3D embroidery creates a stronger raised effect, woven patches can suit lifestyle branding, and rubber badges can provide a more modern retail look.

  • Guidance on cap style, structure, peak, closure and fit
  • Support for embroidery, patches, badges, labels and retail finishing
  • Repeat order planning for teams, companies, events and ecommerce brands

Embroidery, Patches and Labels

Premium Branding Methods for Custom Caps

Embroidery is one of the strongest decoration methods for custom caps because it is durable, professional and highly visible. It works well for corporate logos, team marks, event identities and retail brands.

Raised 3D embroidery can create a bolder front-panel look, while woven patches, rubber patches and badge applications can create a more lifestyle or private label appearance.

For retail or ecommerce headwear, labels, hang tags, visor stickers, packaging and size details can be just as important as the cap itself. These details make the product feel complete and ready for sale.

TShirtSwiss helps align cap structure, branding method and finishing so the final headwear matches your brand and intended use.

Quality Control

Quality Checks for Shape, Stitching, Embroidery and Packing

Headwear quality control focuses on crown shape, panel alignment, peak shape, stitching, closure quality, embroidery placement, patch alignment, label application and packing accuracy.

For private label caps, presentation details such as hang tags, stickers, bags and cartons are part of the final product standard. For team and corporate orders, logo consistency and repeat order matching are especially important.

  • Sample and artwork approval before bulk production
  • Checks for cap shape, construction, branding, labels and packing
  • Delivery preparation for Switzerland, Europe and international destinations
