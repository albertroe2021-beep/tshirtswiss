Private Label Apparel Manufacturing for Swiss Brands

Private label clothing allows a business to sell apparel under its own brand without building a factory, hiring a production team or managing overseas sourcing alone. The right manufacturing partner helps translate an idea into a product specification, sample, production order and finished delivery.

TShirtSwiss supports Swiss apparel brands, ecommerce stores, retailers, gyms, companies, clubs and organisations that want reliable clothing production with clear communication. We help develop custom garments, select fabrics, prepare branding details, manage samples, coordinate production and organise quality control.

Our structure combines Swiss-managed project communication with manufacturing in Thailand. That means clients receive a practical European point of contact while benefiting from Thailand's apparel production capability, supplier networks and experience across sportswear, uniforms, merchandise and retail clothing.

This page is the central hub for our clothing manufacturing capabilities. From t-shirts and polos to hoodies, workwear, healthcare apparel, fightwear, caps and branded merchandise, we help build private label ranges that are ready for real customers.

Product Development

From Idea to Sample to Production

Private label production starts with a clear product direction. Some clients already have a tech pack, sample garment or previous supplier specification. Others need help developing the product from a reference, sketch, moodboard or market idea.

We help define garment type, fit, fabric, construction, branding, trims, labels, packaging and expected order quantities. Samples or pre-production references are then used to confirm the direction before bulk production begins.

  • Support for tech packs, samples, references and new product ideas
  • Fabric, fit, trims, branding and packaging guidance
  • Sampling before production where required by the project
Fabrics, Printing, Labels and Packaging

Everything Your Brand Needs Beyond the Garment

Fabric selection affects the feel, quality perception and cost of a clothing line. We can review cotton, cotton blends, polyester, fleece, pique, softshell, stretch fabrics, performance knits and woven fabrics depending on the product category.

Printing and decoration can include screen printing, embroidery, sublimation, heat transfer and label applications. The right method depends on artwork, fabric, quantity, product type and brand positioning.

Private label clothing also requires branded details. These can include woven neck labels, printed neck labels, care labels, size labels, sleeve labels, hang tags, barcode stickers, polybags, carton markings and retail packing.

These details matter because customers judge the product as a complete brand experience, not only as a piece of fabric. A well-produced garment with poor labels or packaging can feel unfinished, while thoughtful finishing can elevate the entire range.

Quality Control

Quality Checks for Serious Apparel Brands

Quality control is essential in private label clothing because the finished product carries your brand name. Checks can include fabric consistency, measurements, seam quality, print placement, embroidery, label application, colour matching, finishing, folding and packing accuracy.

We help confirm the quality expectations before production so the supplier, client and production team work from the same standard. For ongoing programmes, approved specifications support more consistent repeat orders.

  • Specification and sample approval before production
  • Inline and final checks depending on project scope
  • Packing checks for labels, quantities, cartons and delivery preparation
