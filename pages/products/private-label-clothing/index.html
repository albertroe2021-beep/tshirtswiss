What to Prepare Before Private Label Production

The best private label projects start with a clear product direction. A complete tech pack is useful, but not always required. If you do not have one, references, target samples, measurements, artwork and brand guidelines can help move the project forward.

  • Target customer and brand positioning.
  • Product list, colours, size range and quantities.
  • Reference garments, samples, drawings or tech packs.
  • Fabric preference, weight, stretch or hand-feel direction.
  • Logo files, artwork, label requirements and care label content.
  • Hang tag, sticker, polybag, barcode or carton requirements.
  • Target launch date, delivery destination and fulfilment plan.

Our Private Label Clothing Production Process

We manage each stage with structured communication so your brand understands what is being produced, what needs approval and how the final garment will be delivered.

1. Brand Consultation

We review your product concept, target customer, price point, brand positioning and garment requirements.

2. Specification Planning

We define garment type, fabric, fit, sizing, construction, trims, decoration, labels and packaging.

3. Sampling

Samples allow you to approve fit, fabric feel, branding details and construction before bulk production.

4. Branding and Packaging

We prepare custom labels, hang tags, care labels, size stickers, polybags and packing details.

5. Bulk Production

Your garments are produced according to the approved sample and confirmed specifications.

6. Quality and Shipping

Finished garments are checked, packed and prepared for delivery to your warehouse, store or fulfilment partner.
