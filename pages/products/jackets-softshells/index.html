Use Case Planning

Choose Jackets Around the Environment

A jacket for an office team has different requirements from a jacket for a field crew, sports club, resort team or merchandise collection. The garment should be planned around climate, movement, wash expectations and brand presentation.

  • Corporate jackets should look clean and support subtle branding.
  • Workwear jackets need durability, movement and practical pocket placement.
  • Hospitality layers should be comfortable and match the venue's presentation.
  • Sports club jackets should support team identity and pre- or post-training wear.
  • Event jackets can help staff visibility and weather readiness.
  • Private label jackets need labels, trims and packaging that match retail expectations.
