Merchandise Planning

Promotional Items Should Match the Audience

A successful promotional product is not only about logo placement. The product should match the event, climate, audience, distribution method and brand position.

  • Lightweight t-shirts and caps work well for warm outdoor events.
  • Hoodies and premium tees suit higher-value gifts and merchandise drops.
  • Tote bags are practical for conferences, retail and welcome packs.
  • Polos help event staff and sales teams look more professional.
  • Private label details make merchandise feel more retail-ready.
  • Packing by event, team, branch or recipient group simplifies distribution.
