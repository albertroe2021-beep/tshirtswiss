Workplace Suitability

Plan Workwear Around the Job, Not Just the Logo

Workwear should support the task, environment and team structure. Apparel used by a warehouse team, construction crew, hotel maintenance department or delivery driver may all need different fabrics, colours, pocket choices and branding placements.

  • Breathable fabrics support warm climates and active roles.
  • Durable stitching and stable fabrics help with frequent washing.
  • Bright colours can support team visibility and role recognition where appropriate.
  • Role colours can help identify departments, teams or job functions.
  • Outerwear and layers help teams adapt to outdoor or air-conditioned environments.
  • Replacement planning keeps uniforms consistent as staff numbers change.
