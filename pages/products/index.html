Branding Options

Products Finished with the Details Your Brand Needs

The finished garment is only one part of the product experience. Many clients also need custom labels, woven tags, printed neck labels, hang tags, barcode stickers, polybags, retail packaging, branded cartons or ecommerce-ready packing. These details are especially important for private label clothing, merchandise drops and premium corporate apparel.

Decoration options include screen printing, embroidery, sublimation printing, heat transfer printing and other techniques depending on the garment, fabric and artwork. We help select the right method based on durability, hand feel, colour requirements, quantity and budget. The objective is not simply to place a logo on a product, but to create apparel that feels complete and brand appropriate.
