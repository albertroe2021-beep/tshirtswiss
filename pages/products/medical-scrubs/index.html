Workplace Suitability

Healthcare Uniforms Should Support Long Shifts

Scrubs and healthcare uniforms are worn for long periods and often washed frequently. Planning should consider movement, climate, role, department colours, pocket placement, wash cycles and professional presentation.

  • Stretch fabrics can improve comfort for active roles.
  • Easy-care blends help garments hold shape through repeat laundering.
  • Breathable fabrics are useful in warm clinics and tropical climates.
  • Department colours can support role identification and organisation.
  • Pocket placement should reflect the tools and workflow of the team.
  • Layering pieces can help staff adapt to air-conditioned environments.
