Custom Polo Shirts for Companies, Staff Uniforms and Premium Brand Presentation

A custom polo shirt is often the garment a customer sees first. It can represent a hotel, restaurant, sales team, service company, golf event, sports club, clinic or corporate brand. TShirtSwiss helps Swiss businesses translate those details into a practical production specification. We review the intended use, wearer profile, climate, order quantity, brand style, decoration method and delivery expectations before recommending the right polo construction and fabric direction.

Our production model combines Swiss-managed project handling with apparel manufacturing in Thailand. This gives businesses access to factory-direct polo shirt production while keeping communication, quality checks and planning clear throughout the project.

Whether you need a professional uniform programme, a hospitality polo, an event shirt, retail polo or ongoing company apparel supply, we can manage custom polo manufacturing from concept through to sampling, production, packing and international delivery.

Fabric Selection

Choosing the Right Polo Fabric

Polo shirt fabric affects how the garment feels, how the collar sits, how embroidery performs and how the shirt looks after repeated washing. A hotel uniform may need a breathable fabric with a polished appearance. A golf polo may need stretch and moisture management. A workwear polo may need durability and easy care. A private label polo may need a more premium hand feel and retail-ready finishing.

We help choose fabric based on the intended role of the polo. Cotton pique is a classic choice with texture and breathability. Combed cotton gives a smoother premium feel. Poly-cotton and CVC blends can provide stability, reduced shrinkage and better wash performance. Polyester pique and performance fabrics can be useful for sports, outdoor staff and warmer climates.

  • Fabric recommendations based on uniform use, performance and brand positioning
  • Multiple weight and texture options for corporate, hospitality and retail polos
  • Support for custom colour direction, trim details and repeat production consistency

Embroidery and Branding

Branding Methods for Custom Polo Shirts

Embroidery is one of the most popular decoration methods for polo shirts because it gives a professional, durable and premium finish. It is commonly used for left chest logos, sleeve branding, hospitality uniforms, corporate apparel, golf polos and staff clothing that needs to look polished over time.

Screen printing and heat transfer can also be suitable for certain polo projects, especially when artwork is larger, more graphic or needs to be applied to sleeves or back panels. The correct branding method depends on the fabric, design complexity, quantity, intended use and desired finish.

Custom polo branding can extend beyond the visible logo. We can support woven labels, printed neck labels, branded buttons, contrast plackets, hang tags, care labels and packaging. These details help transform a basic polo into a stronger branded product.

For private label and uniform programmes, consistency matters. Once the embroidery size, stitch direction, placement, thread colour and garment specification are approved, we can support repeat orders that help maintain a consistent professional appearance.

Quality Control

Professional Uniform Quality, Managed Clearly

Polo shirts are often worn by staff in public-facing roles, so inconsistency is easy to notice. Differences in collar shape, embroidery placement, fabric shade, sizing or finishing can affect the way the whole team looks. This is why we treat quality control as part of the production process, not as an afterthought.

Checks can include fabric consistency, collar shape, placket construction, stitching, measurements, embroidery placement, thread colour, label application, folding and packing. For repeat orders, we also focus on maintaining the approved specification wherever possible.

  • Sample and specification approval before bulk production
  • Checks for fit, collar, placket, fabric, embroidery and finishing
  • Packing and delivery preparation for Switzerland and international destinations
