Custom Hoodies and Sweatshirts Made for Premium Brand Presentation

Hoodies and sweatshirts are high-value apparel products. They are used by corporate teams, schools, sports clubs, gyms, streetwear labels, ecommerce brands, event organisers and private label clothing companies because they feel substantial, visible and long-lasting. A well-made hoodie can become a staff favourite, a retail bestseller or a strong merchandise product that people continue wearing long after an event.

Compared with a basic t-shirt, hoodies require more manufacturing decisions. Fabric weight, fleece type, hood shape, drawcords, cuffs, waistband, pocket construction, shrinkage, print placement, embroidery size, label finishing and packaging all affect the final result. TShirtSwiss helps Swiss businesses turn those details into a clear production specification.

Our model combines Swiss-managed communication with apparel manufacturing in Thailand. You receive the benefits of factory-direct hoodie production while keeping the process structured, transparent and aligned with Swiss expectations for quality, reliability and communication.

Whether you need heavyweight private label hoodies, corporate sweatshirts, team apparel, gym merchandise or event fleece products, we can manage the project from concept and sampling through to production, quality control, packing and worldwide delivery.

Fabric Selection

Fleece Weight, Hand Feel and Construction Matter

The fabric choice defines the entire hoodie. French Terry has looped yarns on the inside and can feel cleaner, lighter and more breathable. Brushed fleece has a soft brushed interior that feels warmer and more comfortable in cooler weather. Heavyweight cotton-rich fleece can create a premium streetwear feel, while cotton-poly blends can improve durability, stability and wash performance.

We help choose fleece based on the intended use of the garment. A corporate hoodie may need a polished surface, clean embroidery and stable sizing. A gym hoodie may need comfort and movement. A private label hoodie may need a heavyweight feel, oversized fit and premium finishing details. A school or club hoodie may need reliable repeat production and strong value.

  • French Terry, brushed fleece and heavyweight fabric guidance
  • GSM options for everyday, premium and retail-ready hoodies
  • Support for trims, labels, drawcords, ribbing and repeat production consistency

Printing, Embroidery and Trims

Branding Methods for Custom Hoodies

Hoodies give brands more space and more options than many other garments. Large chest prints, back graphics, sleeve prints, embroidery, patches and neck labels can all be used to create a strong branded product. Screen printing remains a strong option for bold artwork and larger quantities, while embroidery creates a more premium finish for logos, chest details and corporate apparel.

Puff print, heat transfer and patch applications can add a more retail or streetwear feel. Woven patches, rubber badges, sleeve labels, hem labels, custom drawcords, eyelets and branded packaging can all help turn a basic fleece garment into a product that feels intentionally developed.

The best decoration method depends on the fabric, weight, surface texture, artwork size, design complexity, quantity and desired finish. Heavy fleece may require different print handling than lighter French Terry, and embroidery placement should be reviewed carefully to avoid distortion or uncomfortable stitching.

TShirtSwiss helps align the garment, artwork, trim details and finishing so the final hoodie looks consistent with your brand and performs well in real use.

Quality Control

Quality Checks for Heavier Garments and Premium Finishing

Hoodies involve more construction details than many lightweight garments, so quality control needs to look beyond the print. Fabric consistency, shrinkage expectations, hood shape, pocket position, cuffs, waistband, stitching, embroidery placement, label application and packaging all affect the final impression.

For private label or retail hoodies, details such as hang tags, neck labels, folding, bagging and size stickers can be just as important as the garment itself. For corporate or club orders, sizing consistency and repeat order reliability are often the main priority.

  • Sample and specification approval before bulk production
  • Checks for fleece, fit, stitching, trims, decoration and finishing
  • Packing and delivery preparation for Switzerland and international destinations

FAQ

Custom Hoodie Manufacturing FAQ

A few common questions businesses ask before starting a custom hoodie or sweatshirt production project.

Can you manufacture fully custom hoodies?

Yes. We can support custom fleece direction, GSM, fit, colours, drawcords, cuffs, pockets, decoration, labels and packaging depending on quantity and specification.

What fabrics are available for hoodies?

Options can include French Terry, brushed fleece, cotton-rich fleece, cotton-poly blends, heavyweight fleece and recycled options where available.

Can hoodies be embroidered?

Yes. Embroidery is suitable for chest logos, sleeve details and premium branding. The best placement and stitch size depend on the fabric and design.

Can you help with private label hoodies?

Yes. We can support woven labels, neck labels, hang tags, custom trims, packaging and repeat production planning.

Do you ship to Switzerland?

Yes. Orders can be prepared for delivery to Switzerland, Europe and international destinations. Shipping details are reviewed during quotation.
