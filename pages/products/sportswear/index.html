Performance Apparel

Sportswear Designed for Movement, Comfort and Durability

Good sportswear is not just a garment with an athletic look. It must support movement, manage heat and moisture, feel comfortable against the skin and maintain its shape after repeated use. We help clients choose fabrics and construction details that match the intended sport, activity level, climate and brand positioning.

For fitness brands and activewear labels, the product also needs to look strong online and feel premium when customers receive it. For gyms and sports teams, consistency, durability and brand impact are often the priority. TShirt Swiss adapts the production plan to the real purpose of the garment.

Process

Our Sportswear Production Process

We manage sportswear production with a clear process from product planning to delivery, making sure fabric, fit, decoration and finishing are aligned before bulk manufacturing starts.

1

Performance Brief

We define the garment type, activity, fit, fabric needs, branding and production goal.

2

Fabric Selection

We advise on polyester, spandex blends, dry-tech fabrics, mesh, compression and stretch materials.

3

Sample Development

Samples allow you to approve fit, movement, fabric feel, print effect and construction.

4

Decoration Planning

We select sublimation, screen printing, heat transfer, embroidery or labels depending on the garment.

5

Bulk Production

Cutting, sewing, decoration and finishing are managed to the approved specification.

6

Inspection and Shipping

Finished sportswear is checked, packed and prepared for worldwide delivery.
