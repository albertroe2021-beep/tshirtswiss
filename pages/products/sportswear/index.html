Fabric Guide

Sportswear Fabric Types and Benefits

Fabric selection should be based on activity, climate, stretch needs, artwork method, wash frequency and target price point.

Dry-Tech Polyester

Lightweight and quick drying for training shirts, race apparel, teamwear and warm climates.

Polyester-Spandex

Supports stretch and recovery for compression tops, activewear and rashguards.

Compression Knits

Useful for close-fitting performance garments and private label activewear.

Mesh Panels

Can improve airflow in high-heat areas of training tops and active garments.

Sublimation Polyester

Ideal for full-colour teamwear, rashguards, MMA shorts and technical artwork.

Cotton-Rich Blends

Useful for lifestyle gym apparel, fan merchandise and off-duty sportswear.
