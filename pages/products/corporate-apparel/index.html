Programme Planning

Corporate Apparel Should Be Easy to Roll Out and Reorder

Corporate clients often need apparel across departments, branches, job roles, sizes and repeat orders. The best programmes document the approved garment, colours, logo placement, size range and packing method so future production can stay consistent.

  • Define garment types by role: office, field, sales, events or management.
  • Choose fabrics around climate, movement and wash frequency.
  • Use consistent logo placement across polos, jackets, caps and shirts.
  • Plan size ratios and staff allocation before production.
  • Pack by department, branch, staff group, event team or location.
  • Document specifications for reorders and future staff growth.
