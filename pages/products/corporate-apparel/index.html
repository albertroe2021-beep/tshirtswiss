Fabric and Wearability

Choosing Apparel Staff Will Actually Wear

Corporate apparel needs to look professional, but it also needs to be comfortable enough for daily use. A garment that looks good on the sample table but feels restrictive, overheats, wrinkles easily or washes poorly can quickly become a problem across a team.

We help select fabrics based on the job role and environment. Office teams may need easy-care shirts and refined polos. Sales staff may need travel-friendly fabrics. Hospitality groups may need durable garments that wash frequently. Outdoor or event teams may need jackets, softshells or fleece layers that look coordinated with the rest of the uniform.

  • Fabric recommendations based on role, climate, presentation and wash requirements
  • Coordinated product planning across polos, shirts, jackets and supporting garments
  • Support for repeat ordering, staff sizing and consistent brand presentation

Embroidery and Professional Branding

Branding Methods for Corporate Apparel

Corporate apparel usually needs branding that feels professional rather than overly promotional. Embroidery is a strong choice for polos, jackets, softshells, aprons and premium uniforms because it gives a durable, polished finish. It works well for chest logos, sleeve details and subtle brand placement.

For shirts and lighter fabrics, decoration needs to be reviewed carefully so it does not affect comfort or appearance. In some cases, woven labels, tonal embroidery, printed neck labels or subtle trims can communicate the brand more effectively than a large visible logo.

Brand consistency matters across departments and locations. Once logo size, thread colour, placement and garment specification are approved, we can support repeat orders to help maintain a consistent appearance over time.

TShirtSwiss helps align garment choice, fabric, branding method and finishing details so the final apparel feels professional, appropriate and durable enough for regular use.

Quality Control

Consistency Across Teams, Sizes and Repeat Orders

Corporate apparel programmes depend on consistency. If one department receives a different shade, a different logo placement, a different fit or a different finishing standard, the brand presentation suffers. This is why quality control is especially important for staff apparel and uniform programmes.

Checks can include fabric consistency, sizing, stitching, embroidery placement, label application, shade matching, packing and department grouping. For repeat orders, approved specifications help keep future production aligned with earlier deliveries wherever possible.

  • Sample and specification approval before bulk production
  • Checks for fit, fabric, branding, measurements and finishing
  • Packing and delivery preparation for Switzerland and international destinations
