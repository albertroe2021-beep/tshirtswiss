Fabric Selection

The Right Fabric Changes Everything

The fabric is one of the most important decisions in custom t-shirt manufacturing. It affects comfort, durability, print finish, wash performance, shrinkage, drape and perceived quality. A promotional t-shirt for a short event may require a different fabric from a premium retail t-shirt or a staff uniform that is washed every week.

We help choose materials based on the intended use of the garment. Cotton and combed cotton are popular for soft everyday wear. Organic and recycled cotton can support more responsible product stories. Poly-cotton and CVC blends add durability and stability for workwear or regular washing. Polyester and micro-polyester can be suitable for sports, gym and warmer-climate use.

  • Fabric recommendations based on use, budget and brand positioning
  • Multiple GSM options for lightweight, midweight and premium t-shirts
  • Support for custom colour direction and repeat production consistency

Printing and Branding

Decoration Methods for Custom T-Shirts

Decoration method should be selected after reviewing the artwork, fabric, quantity, budget and desired finish. Screen printing remains one of the strongest options for bold, durable designs and larger production quantities. It is often used for company logos, event shirts, club merchandise and private label graphics where colour strength and wash durability matter.

Embroidery can create a more premium or corporate feel, especially for small logos, chest branding and higher-value uniform programmes. Heat transfer and transfer-style decoration can suit complex artwork, smaller runs or names and numbers. Sublimation works best on polyester fabrics and is often used for sportswear or all-over designs.

Branding can also go beyond the front print. We can support printed neck labels, woven labels, care labels, sleeve details, hem labels, hang tags and custom packaging. These details are especially important for private label t-shirts, ecommerce brands and retail-ready apparel.

The best custom t-shirt projects are not just about placing artwork on fabric. They combine the correct garment construction, decoration process and finishing details so the final product feels intentional and aligned with your brand.

FAQ

Custom T-Shirt Manufacturing FAQ

A few common questions businesses ask before starting a custom t-shirt production project.

Can you manufacture fully custom t-shirts?

Yes. We can support custom fabric direction, garment fit, GSM, colours, decoration, labels and packaging. The level of customisation depends on the quantity, specification and production requirements.

Do you offer printing and embroidery?

Yes. Depending on the design and fabric, options can include screen printing, embroidery, heat transfer, sublimation, printed neck labels and other branding methods.

Can you help with private label t-shirts?

Yes. We can support woven labels, care labels, neck labels, hang tags, packaging and repeat production planning for private label and ecommerce apparel brands.

Do you ship to Switzerland?

Yes. Orders can be prepared for delivery to Switzerland, Europe and other international destinations. Shipping details are reviewed during the quotation stage.

Can you help choose the right fabric?

Yes. We recommend fabrics based on how the t-shirt will be used, the desired feel, print method, budget, durability expectations and brand positioning.

Can we reorder the same t-shirt later?

Yes. For repeat orders, we aim to maintain the approved specification, including fabric direction, decoration details, sizing and finishing where available.
