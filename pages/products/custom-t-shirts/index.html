Printing and Decoration Options

Screen Printing, Embroidery, Sublimation and Transfers

Different t-shirt designs require different application methods. Screen printing is ideal for many corporate, merchandise and promotional t-shirt orders because it provides strong colour and good durability at scale. Embroidery gives a premium finish for subtle logos, although it is more commonly used on polos, caps and heavier garments. Heat transfer printing can work well for names, numbers, smaller runs and certain technical fabrics. Sublimation is suitable for polyester performance t-shirts and full-colour all-over designs.

We do not recommend decoration methods based only on what is easiest to produce. We consider the fabric, artwork, garment use, wash requirements, quantity and brand positioning. A premium private label t-shirt may need a soft-hand print and custom neck label. A staff uniform may need durable branding that holds up to frequent washing. A promotional event t-shirt may need efficient production and bold visibility.

Our team can review your artwork and advise the most suitable method before production begins.

Fabric Options

Custom T-Shirt Fabric Composition

Fabric choice is one of the most important decisions in t-shirt manufacturing. It affects hand feel, breathability, shrinkage, durability, print result, price and customer perception. We can help you choose from a wide range of fabric compositions depending on whether your priority is softness, structure, performance, sustainability or budget.

  • 100% Cotton: a classic breathable choice for everyday custom t-shirts.
  • Combed Cotton: smoother and more refined for premium t-shirt production.
  • Semi-Combed Cotton: a practical balance between comfort and cost efficiency.
  • Organic Cotton: suitable for brands with eco-conscious positioning.
  • Recycled Cotton: an option for sustainability-focused programmes.
  • Cotton Polyester Blends: combine comfort with improved durability and shape retention.
  • CVC Blends: popular for soft, reliable everyday t-shirts.
  • Polyester Performance Fabrics: suitable for sports, events and active use.
  • Micro Polyester: smooth, lightweight and useful for technical t-shirt applications.
  • Cotton Spandex or Lycra Blends: add stretch and movement for fitted or active styles.
  • Dry-Tech Fabrics: designed for moisture management and warm-weather use.
  • Antibacterial Polyester Options: useful for activewear, uniforms and demanding environments.
