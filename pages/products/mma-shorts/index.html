Product Strategy

MMA Shorts Should Balance Movement, Durability and Brand Identity

MMA shorts are a technical apparel product. Buyers should think beyond the artwork and consider waistband security, stretch placement, fabric hand feel, leg opening, seam strength and how the garment pairs with rashguards or team shirts.

For gyms, shorts create a unified training look. For ecommerce brands, they can become a retail product. For events and athlete drops, they create strong visual identity and sponsor space.

Fabric Guide

MMA Shorts Fabric Types and Benefits

Waistband, Length and Panel Placement Matter

The construction of MMA shorts should support movement while keeping the garment secure and comfortable. Waistband style, closure system, side slit height, panel placement and short length should be selected around the intended use.
