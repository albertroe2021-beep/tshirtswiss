How We Work

A Clear Production Process Without Guesswork

Every successful apparel order starts with clear information. Before production begins, we review the garment type, quantity, target use, fabric expectations, colour direction, size range, decoration method, packaging requirements, delivery destination and deadline.

This planning stage helps prevent common production problems such as unsuitable fabric, unclear logo placement, inconsistent sizing, missed packaging details or unrealistic lead times. For clients with a tech pack, we review the specification and advise on manufacturability. For clients without a full technical file, we help define the details required to move forward.

The goal is simple: make sure the garment is planned correctly before the factory starts cutting, sewing, printing or packing. That structure is what allows us to support corporate apparel, uniforms, workwear, sportswear, promotional merchandise and private label clothing with a consistent process.

Production Steps

From Brief to Finished Garments

The exact process depends on the product, but most projects move through the same core stages. Each stage is designed to confirm the right details before the next step begins.

Consultation

We clarify your product, quantity, usage, target market, budget, branding and delivery deadline.

Specification

Fabric, measurements, colours, trims, labels, decoration and packing details are defined.

Sampling

Where needed, samples confirm fit, hand feel, artwork placement and final presentation.

Production

Approved garments move into cutting, sewing, decoration, finishing and production management.

Quality Control

Measurements, stitching, decoration, labels, packing and general consistency are checked.

Shipping

Finished orders are packed and prepared for delivery to Switzerland, Europe and worldwide markets.

Factory Coordination

Manufacturing Managed With Structure and Accountability

Producing apparel internationally can become difficult when communication is unclear or specifications are incomplete. TShirt Swiss helps reduce that risk by managing the production process with clear approval points and practical communication.

We coordinate product details before production, confirm decoration requirements, guide fabric and construction choices where needed, and make sure packing expectations are understood before orders are shipped.

Our Thailand production base gives clients access to skilled garment manufacturing, while Swiss management provides the project discipline, documentation and quality mindset that international buyers expect.

Quality Control

Quality Is Checked Before, During and After Production

Quality control is more effective when it happens throughout the order, not only at the end. We look at fabric suitability, measurements, stitching, print or embroidery placement, colour consistency, trims, labels, finishing and packaging.

For private label garments, we also check the retail presentation, including labels, hang tags and packing. For uniforms and corporate apparel, we focus on consistency across sizes, departments and repeat orders.

This process helps protect your brand and reduces the chance of costly surprises after production is complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

Production FAQ

What is the first step in starting production?

The first step is to send your garment type, quantity, artwork or logo, target delivery date and destination. Reference images, samples or tech packs are also helpful when available.

Can I approve a sample before bulk production?

Yes. Sampling can be used to confirm fit, fabric feel, logo placement, finishing and packaging before bulk production starts.

What decoration methods do you support?

We support common apparel decoration methods including screen printing, embroidery, sublimation, heat transfer, woven labels, printed labels, hang tags and packaging solutions.

How does quality control work?

Quality checks focus on measurements, stitching, fabric condition, decoration placement, colour consistency, labels, finishing and packing. Checks are managed at multiple points rather than only at the end.

Can you manage repeat orders?

Yes. Approved garment specifications, fabrics, colours, artwork placement, labels and packing details can be documented so future reorders are easier to repeat consistently.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes. Orders can be prepared for worldwide delivery, including Switzerland, Europe, Australia, Asia and other international markets.
