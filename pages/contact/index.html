Before You Contact Us

Information That Helps Us Respond Accurately

Apparel manufacturing depends on details. Two t-shirts can look similar but have very different prices depending on fabric weight, fit, printing method, labels, packaging and quantity. A private label hoodie requires different planning from a promotional event t-shirt. A healthcare uniform has different requirements from a sportswear garment. The more context you share, the better advice we can provide.

Product Information

Tell us what garment you want to produce, how it will be used and whether you have a reference product or design direction.

Quantity and Sizing

Share estimated quantities by style, colour and size. This affects production planning, pricing and decoration options.

Branding Requirements

Send logos, artwork, label requirements, packaging ideas and any specific brand guidelines you want us to follow.

Timeline and Destination

Let us know your target delivery date and shipping destination so we can advise realistic production and logistics timing.

Why Clients Contact TShirt Swiss

Clients contact us when they want the production advantages of Thailand with the structure and accountability of Swiss management.
