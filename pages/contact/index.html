Contact FAQ

Common Questions Before You Enquire

What details should I send for a quote?

Send the garment type, quantity, artwork or logo, preferred fabric, size range, colours, delivery destination and deadline. Reference photos, tech packs and previous samples are also helpful.

Can you manufacture private label clothing?

Yes. We support private label manufacturing, including product development, sampling, custom labels, hang tags, packaging and repeat production planning.

Do you ship to Switzerland?

Yes. Orders can be prepared for delivery to Switzerland, Europe and other international destinations. Shipping details depend on order size, destination and timeline.

Can I order samples before bulk production?

Samples can be used where appropriate to confirm fit, fabric, construction, artwork placement, labels and finishing before bulk production begins.

What products can you manufacture?

We support custom t-shirts, polos, hoodies, workwear, corporate apparel, healthcare uniforms, sportswear, rash guards, MMA shorts, Muay Thai shorts, caps, tote bags, promotional merchandise and private label clothing.

What decoration methods do you offer?

We can support screen printing, embroidery, sublimation printing, heat transfer printing, custom labels, hang tags and packaging solutions depending on the product and artwork.
