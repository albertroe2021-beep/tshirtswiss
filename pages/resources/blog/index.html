Practical Insights for Custom Apparel Manufacturing

The TShirt Swiss blog is designed for companies, brands and organisations planning apparel production. We cover the decisions that affect the final result: fabric selection, garment fit, sampling, printing, embroidery, private label branding, packaging, quality control and international shipping.

Our articles are written from a production perspective. Instead of generic fashion content, we focus on the practical details that help you prepare better briefs, avoid common manufacturing mistakes and build stronger garments with a Swiss-managed apparel manufacturer in Thailand.

A strong production brief can save time, reduce mistakes and make quoting more accurate. Before contacting a manufacturer, it helps to understand the difference between garment types, fabric compositions, decoration techniques, custom labelling options and shipping requirements. The blog will continue to expand around these topics so clients can make more informed decisions.

Whether you are ordering corporate uniforms, launching an ecommerce apparel brand, planning event merchandise or developing performance sportswear, the right information at the start of the project leads to a better final product.
