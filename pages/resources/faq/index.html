Ordering and Planning

Production Basics

What types of apparel do you manufacture?

We manufacture custom t-shirts, polo shirts, hoodies, sweatshirts, corporate apparel, workwear, healthcare uniforms, medical scrubs, sportswear, rashguards, MMA shorts, Muay Thai shorts, caps, tote bags, promotional merchandise and private label clothing.

Do you work with startups and new brands?

Yes. We can support new brands with product development, fabric guidance, sampling, custom labels, packaging and production planning. We also work with established companies, agencies, corporate buyers and distributors.

What information do you need for a quote?

Please provide garment type, quantity, sizes, colours, fabric preference, artwork or logo, decoration method, label or packaging requirements, deadline and shipping destination. Samples, tech packs or reference images are also useful.

Do you have minimum order quantities?

Minimum quantities depend on the product, fabric, decoration method and level of customisation. A simple branded t-shirt order is different from a fully custom private label garment. Send your details and we will advise what is realistic.

Sampling and Customisation

Before Bulk Production

Can you make samples before production?

Yes. Sampling is recommended for private label clothing, new styles, technical garments and orders where fit, fabric or decoration must be approved before bulk production. Sampling helps reduce risk and creates a clear production reference.

Can you copy or improve an existing garment?

We can work from a reference garment or sample and help develop a similar or improved production specification. We do not copy protected designs, but we can use references to understand fit, fabric, construction and quality expectations.

Can you add custom labels and packaging?

Yes. We can support woven labels, printed neck labels, care labels, hang tags, size stickers, polybags, branded packaging and carton preparation for private label, retail, ecommerce and premium corporate projects.

Which printing methods do you offer?

Options include screen printing, embroidery, sublimation printing and heat transfer printing. The best method depends on fabric, artwork, quantity, durability requirements and garment use.

Production and Delivery

Quality, Timelines and Shipping

How long does apparel production take?

Lead time depends on sampling, fabric availability, quantity, decoration complexity, packaging and shipping destination. We provide realistic timelines during the quotation stage so clients can plan properly.

How do you manage quality control?

We check specifications, fabric suitability, measurements, stitching, print or embroidery placement, labels, finishing and packing. Quality control is managed throughout the process, not only after production is complete.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We support worldwide shipping from Thailand to Switzerland, Europe, Australia, Asia, North America and other destinations. Shipping options depend on order size, deadline and delivery requirements.

Why choose TShirt Swiss instead of a basic supplier?

TShirt Swiss combines Thai manufacturing capability with Swiss-managed communication and accountability. We focus on premium production, clear specifications, quality control and long-term client relationships rather than anonymous low-cost sourcing.
