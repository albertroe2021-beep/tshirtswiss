Swiss-Managed Apparel Resources

Resources Written for Real Manufacturing Decisions

Many apparel guides focus on trends, but production buyers need practical manufacturing information. A successful custom garment order depends on fabric selection, measurements, artwork placement, decoration method, labels, hang tags, packing, quality control and delivery timing.

TShirt Swiss resources are written from the perspective of Swiss-managed apparel manufacturing in Thailand. That means the advice is connected to real production decisions, not theory. Whether you are preparing a corporate uniform order, launching a private label range or organising event merchandise, our goal is to help you ask better questions before requesting a quote.

Swiss businesses often want the accountability and clarity of a local-style partner while still accessing the production value of Thailand. Our resource centre supports that decision-making process by explaining how production steps connect, what details affect price, and how to reduce risk before bulk manufacturing begins.

Use this hub to explore product planning, services, industries, quality control, packaging and quote preparation before starting your next project.

Manufacturing Capability

Learn What Goes Into a Successful Apparel Order

Many production issues come from unclear specifications at the beginning of a project. A garment quote is not only about quantity and logo placement. It can involve fabric composition, GSM, fit, sizing, stitching, print method, embroidery density, label type, packaging format, carton packing, delivery destination and quality tolerance.

Our resource content explains these decisions in a practical way, helping you prepare better briefs and compare manufacturing options more realistically. Whether you are ordering corporate uniforms, launching a private label collection or planning event merchandise, the right preparation saves time, reduces risk and improves the final product.

  • Fabric and GSM planning
  • Sampling and approval
  • Screen printing and embroidery
  • Sublimation and heat transfer
  • Labels, hang tags and packaging
  • Quality control and shipping

Prepare Better Quote Requests

Use the Resource Hub Before You Ask for Pricing

A strong quote request gives the manufacturer enough detail to understand the actual order. Quantity alone is not enough. Pricing can change depending on fabric type, garment complexity, print positions, embroidery size, labels, packaging, sampling, packing method and shipping destination.

Before requesting a quote, review the relevant product, service or industry pages. This helps you decide whether you need a simple custom t-shirt order, a private label product, a uniform programme, a sportswear range, a promotional merchandise pack or a full OEM production route.

Better preparation helps us respond faster and more accurately. It also helps you compare suppliers more fairly, because each quote can be based on the same specification rather than vague assumptions.

When you are ready, send your garment type, quantity, artwork, timeline and delivery destination through the Request a Quote page.
