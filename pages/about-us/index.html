Our Story

Built for Brands That Need More Than a Cheap Supplier

TShirt Swiss was created for clients who want a serious manufacturing partner, not a basic print shop or anonymous factory. Many businesses begin their sourcing journey by searching for the lowest possible unit price. They quickly discover that cheap production can create expensive problems: unclear communication, inconsistent sizing, weak fabrics, missed deadlines, poor finishing and orders that do not match the approved sample.

We take a different approach. Our company is Swiss owned and managed, with production based in Thailand. This gives our clients the advantage of access to experienced garment production while maintaining the project discipline, documentation and quality mindset associated with Swiss business culture. We focus on long-term manufacturing relationships, repeatable quality and brand-safe execution.

From the first enquiry, our team works to understand your commercial goals. Are you launching a private label clothing brand? Ordering premium corporate uniforms? Creating event merchandise? Developing technical sportswear? Each project requires different fabrics, patterns, decoration methods, packaging and delivery planning. We help you make those decisions clearly so your order is built correctly from the beginning.

Swiss Standards

Precision, Communication and Accountability

Swiss management is not just a marketing phrase. It shapes the way we handle specifications, sampling, approvals, production updates and quality control. We believe clients should know what is happening with their order, what decisions need to be made, what costs are involved and what timeline is realistic.

Our production process is built around clear information. We confirm garment type, fabric composition, GSM, colour references, sizing, trims, labels, print or embroidery details, packaging, quantity, shipping destination and deadlines before production moves forward. This reduces misunderstanding and helps protect both the client and the final product.

Quality is checked at multiple stages, not only at the end. We look at fabric suitability, measurements, stitching, decoration placement, colour accuracy, finishing and packing. For businesses that rely on their apparel to represent their brand, these details matter.

How We Work

Clear Steps, Better Decisions, Fewer Surprises

We help clients move from idea to finished garment through a structured process. The first step is understanding the product, quantity, purpose, timeline and delivery destination. From there, we advise on fabric, fit, decoration, labels, packaging and sampling.

When details are approved, bulk production can be managed with clearer expectations. Finished goods are checked, packed and prepared for shipment. This process gives buyers better control than ordering from a supplier that simply says yes without reviewing the real production requirements.
