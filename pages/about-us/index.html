Our Story

Built for Brands That Need More Than a Cheap Supplier

TShirt Swiss was created for clients who want a serious manufacturing partner, not a basic print shop or anonymous factory. Many businesses begin their sourcing journey by searching for the lowest possible unit price. They quickly discover that cheap production often creates expensive problems: unclear communication, inconsistent sizing, weak fabrics, missed deadlines, poor finishing and orders that do not match the approved sample.

We take a different approach. Our company is Swiss owned and managed, with production based in Thailand. This gives our clients the advantage of direct access to experienced garment production while maintaining the project discipline, documentation and quality mindset associated with Swiss business culture. We focus on long-term manufacturing relationships, repeatable quality and brand-safe execution.

From the first enquiry, our team works to understand your commercial goals. Are you launching a private label clothing brand? Ordering premium corporate uniforms? Creating event merchandise? Developing technical sportswear? Each project requires different fabrics, patterns, decoration methods, packaging and delivery planning. We help you make those decisions clearly so your order is built correctly from the beginning.

Swiss Standards

Precision, Communication and Accountability

Swiss management is not just a marketing phrase. It shapes the way we handle specifications, sampling, approvals, production updates and quality control. We believe clients should know what is happening with their order, what decisions need to be made, what costs are involved and what timeline is realistic.

Our production process is built around clear information. We confirm garment type, fabric composition, GSM, colour references, sizing, trims, labels, print or embroidery details, packaging, quantity, shipping destination and deadlines before production moves forward. This reduces misunderstanding and helps protect both the client and the final product.

Quality is checked at multiple stages, not only at the end. We look at fabric suitability, measurements, stitching, decoration placement, colour accuracy, finishing and packing. For businesses that rely on their apparel to represent their brand, these details matter. A uniform, t-shirt, hoodie or private label product is often the first physical touchpoint a customer has with your company.

What We Produce

Custom Clothing, Corporate Apparel and Private Label Production

Our manufacturing capabilities cover a wide range of apparel categories, including custom t-shirts, polo shirts, hoodies, sweatshirts, jackets, workwear, healthcare uniforms, medical scrubs, sportswear, rashguards, MMA shorts, Muay Thai shorts, caps, tote bags and promotional merchandise. We also support OEM clothing production and private label manufacturing for brands that need custom designs, labels, packaging and collection development.

We work with clients at different stages of growth. Some come to us with complete tech packs and established sizing. Others have an idea, a logo, a sketch or an existing sample they want to improve. Our role is to guide the project into a manufacturable product that looks good, fits properly, performs well and reflects the positioning of the brand.

Because we serve international clients, we understand that apparel requirements vary by market. Swiss and European corporate buyers often prioritise consistency, clean finishing and reliable sizing. Ecommerce brands may need strong visual impact, custom branding and packaging. Sports and fitness clients need performance fabrics and durable construction. Hospitality and healthcare teams require comfort, washability and professional presentation. We adapt the solution to the end use.
