Why TShirt Swiss

Swiss Quality Standards. Thai Manufacturing Expertise.

TShirt Swiss is positioned for companies that want more than the cheapest garment supplier. We work with brands, agencies, corporates and organisations that need apparel to represent them professionally. A t-shirt, polo, hoodie or uniform is not just a product; it is a visible expression of your brand.

Our Swiss-managed process helps avoid the common problems that happen when sourcing overseas: unclear communication, inconsistent sizing, poor decoration placement, low-grade fabrics and missed expectations. We define the project, confirm the details, create samples when required and manage production with a quality-first mindset.

Thailand provides an excellent base for premium garment manufacturing. It offers skilled sewing, flexible production options, strong decoration capabilities and international logistics. When combined with Swiss oversight, it gives clients a reliable alternative to anonymous mass production.
