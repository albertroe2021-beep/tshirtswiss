A Transparent Manufacturing Process for Apparel Production

International apparel manufacturing works best when the process is clear. TShirtSwiss.ch manages each order through defined steps: consultation, specification review, artwork planning, sampling where required, material sourcing, production, quality control, packing and shipping. This structure helps Swiss businesses understand what happens at each stage and what information is needed before production begins.

The process starts with your brief. We review garment type, fabric direction, quantities, artwork, sizes, labels, packing needs, delivery market and deadline. We then advise on production options and, where needed, develop samples or mockups for approval. Once the product is confirmed, production is coordinated with our Thailand manufacturing base and checked against the agreed specification.

Quality control is built into the process rather than left until the end. Measurements, stitching, branding placement, colour consistency, trims, labels, packaging and carton details can all be reviewed before orders leave the factory. This matters for Switzerland-focused apparel orders where the garments need to represent a company, team, brand or retail product professionally.

To learn more about the full manufacturing workflow, visit our Production Process page. It explains how Swiss-managed oversight and Thailand-based garment production work together to support reliable custom apparel manufacturing for Swiss companies.
